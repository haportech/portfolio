About Me

I'm Hapor — a full-stack developer, sysadmin, and security practitioner based in Vientiane, Laos. Also known as HaporTech or Jack. I build production systems from the ground up — from real-time collaboration tools and monitoring dashboards to full-stack web applications and game servers.

My workflow bridges development and operations: I architect Node.js backends, wire up SQLite/PostgreSQL, containerize with Docker, and self-host on ARM infrastructure. Security isn't an afterthought — it's built into every layer.

When I'm not deploying or debugging, I'm exploring distributed systems, home lab automation, and the intersection of developer tooling with real-world reliability.
